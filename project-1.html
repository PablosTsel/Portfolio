All In One Sports

The website I created functions as a dynamic search engine specifically tailored for sports enthusiasts, with a focus on basketball, football, and Formula 1 (F1). The platform is designed to provide users with a comprehensive and up-to-date database of information related to these sports.

(It might take 1-2 minutes to load)

Live Link

Project Overview

At its core, the website leverages a powerful sports API, ensuring that the data available is not only extensive but also current. This API integration allows for real-time updates on various aspects of the sports, including player statistics, team rankings, upcoming fixtures, and race schedules. The website's design emphasizes ease of navigation, ensuring that users can seamlessly switch between sports and find the information they're interested in. It also includes interactive elements like following people and liking drivers. I used MongoDb as our Database Storage where we can see who signe up

In F1 you have information about the track of upcoming races and also information about every driver when clicking his card. In basketball you can find information about the NBA standings from the past 4 years and also past, live and upcoming baketball games. In the football section you can look up players and also get informed about live maches.
