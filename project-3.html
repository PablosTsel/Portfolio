NBA Dashboard

NBA interactive dashboard cointaining information about different basketball metrics

Download Excel

Project Overview

The excel file contains various types of data and statistics related to NBA players for the years 2015-2017. It uses data from the years 2015-2017 and has multiple sections with different types of data visualizations and tables. The spreadsheet uses tables and graphs to compare and contrast different aspects of player performance, such as shooting efficiency, player efficiency rating, free throw percentage, and overall performance scores. It also shows the average statistics by player positions, such as point guard, shooting guard, small forward, power forward, and center. The spreadsheet also displays the best players over time, based on their overall performance scores, in a line graph at the bottom middle section. The spreadsheet provides a comprehensive and detailed analysis of NBA player performance over a span of 3 years.
